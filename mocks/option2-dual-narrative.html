📚 Concept: Story Structure
Tier 1 — Foundational

"Every story needs a beginning, middle, and end — just like a sandwich! The bread is your beginning and end, and all the yummy stuff in the middle is where the adventure happens! 🥪"

Tier 2 — Developing

"Let's map your narrative arc: exposition sets the scene, rising action builds tension, the climax is your story's turning point, and the resolution ties it all together. Where is your story's climax right now?"

Tier 3 — Advanced

"Consider how non-linear narrative structure creates tension in your opening. By beginning in medias res, you force the reader to piece together context — a technique Toni Morrison uses to powerful effect."

Practice that feels like play

Smart hints guide them forward without giving away the answer. Three levels of support, always just enough.

Light "What might your character be feeling when they see the door? Think about a time you felt surprised."
Medium "Try using a feeling word like 'nervous' or 'curious.' Where in their body might they feel it?"
Direct "How about starting with: 'Her heart raced as...' and then describing what she did next?"
