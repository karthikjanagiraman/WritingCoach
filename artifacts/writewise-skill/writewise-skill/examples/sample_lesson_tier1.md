## Key Teaching Moments in This Example

1. **Instruction Phase**
   - Hook + Concept + Examples + Check (complete structure)
   - Two examples provided for variety
   - Simple comprehension question
   - Immediate positive reinforcement when answered correctly

2. **Guided Practice Phase**
   - Started with simple exercise (rewrite boring sentence)
   - Light scaffold when first attempt missed the mark
   - Celebrated success enthusiastically
   - Pushed for second attempt to confirm understanding
   - Clear transition to assessment

3. **Assessment Phase**
   - Clear task with checklist
   - Redirected when student asked for help (didn't provide ideas)
   - Let student work independently

4. **Feedback Phase**
   - Started with genuine enthusiasm
   - Quoted specific text from submission
   - Explained WHY the hook worked
   - ONE growth area with concrete suggestion
   - Ended with encouragement
