Concept Comparison

Criteria Artisan Craft Cosmic Explorer Crystalline
Parent Appeal Certificate aesthetic parents want to share Fun but reads as “game” Beautiful but abstract
Age Range (7–15) Medals are universally understood Strong for all ages May feel “fantasy game” for teens
Brand Alignment Writing = craft, badges = artifacts No intrinsic link to writing Learning as geology — a stretch
Differentiation Unique in EdTech space Space theme is common Gem theme is common in games
Performance Lightweight CSS gradients Star fields + animations can lag Backdrop-filter can be heavy
Shareability Certificate share card is premium Discovery card is novel Crystal card is decorative
★

Recommended: Artisan Craft

The Artisan Craft concept directly connects to WriteWhiz’ core identity. Writing is a craft. Medals and certificates are universally understood by children and parents alike. The certificate-style share cards look prestigious in social feeds — not like game screenshots. The metallic gradient CSS is lightweight, and the concept scales elegantly from playful bronze coins for 7-year-olds to museum-quality commemorative medallions for teenagers. No other EdTech product uses this visual language for badges.
