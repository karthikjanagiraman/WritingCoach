Recommended: Ink in Motion

  • Builds on the existing ink-splat sticker visual language already established in the product
  • Tier-adaptive logo means each age group feels the brand is theirs -- Explorer, Adventurer, Trailblazer
  • The "z" splash flourish is uniquely ownable -- no other ed-tech brand has this mark
  • All fonts (Literata, Nunito, Sora, DM Sans) are already loaded -- zero new dependencies
  • Ink is the most universal symbol of writing, instantly understood by kids and parents alike
