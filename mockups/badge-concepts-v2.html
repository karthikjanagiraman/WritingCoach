Recommended: Magic Cards

Trading cards are the most proven collectible format for kids ages 7–15. The holographic borders and character art create real "show your friend" moments.

  • Pokémon-style rarity tiers kids already understand
  • Characters personify writing concepts (Scribble Sprite = first idea, Story Phoenix = mastery)
  • Face-down locked cards create "unboxing" anticipation
  • Card flip reveal = most exciting unlock animation
  • Holographic border effects on Epic/Legendary are genuinely "WHOA"
