Usage Guidelines

Rules for maintaining brand consistency across applications.

Logo Usage

  • Always maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "W" in "Write"
  • Minimum display size: 120px wide for digital, 30mm for print
  • Use the full-color logo on light backgrounds (#FFF9F0 or white)
  • Use the reversed logo on dark backgrounds (#2D3436 or darker)
  • Use the monochrome logo for single-color print applications

Icon Usage

  • Use the 512px icon for app stores, splash screens, and marketing
  • Use the 192px icon for home screen icons and smaller displays
  • Use the favicon for browser tabs and bookmarks
  • Use tier-specific variants only within the context of that tier's experience

The Ink Splash

  • The ink splash is the core brand element — it represents creative energy and the act of writing
  • Droplets represent individual skills breaking free and taking flight
  • Motion dashes convey energy and momentum in the learning journey
  • The glossy highlight gives depth and a tactile, "wet ink" feel
  • Never flatten the splash into a simple circle or geometric shape

Do

  • Use SVG format for all digital applications
  • Maintain the organic, hand-crafted feel of the ink splash
  • Pair "Write" (serif) with "Whiz" (rounded bold) in the wordmark
  • Include the z-flourish splash when space permits
  • Use tier-appropriate colors for tier-specific contexts

Don't

  • Stretch, skew, or rotate the logo or icon
  • Change the colors of the ink splash or droplets
  • Add drop shadows, outlines, or effects to the logo
  • Place the logo on busy or patterned backgrounds
  • Use the Tier 1 coral palette for Tier 3 contexts (or mix tiers)
